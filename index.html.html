✦ Who We Are

A youth-led movement rooted in compassion and care

Hearts of Aloha is a youth-driven community service organization dedicated to nurturing life in all its forms — from the elder animals who have no voice, to the vulnerable newborns who need protection, to the streets and green spaces that bind our communities together.

We believe that a thriving community begins with the small, everyday acts of kindness: feeding a stray animal, tidying a neighborhood street, planting a sapling, or simply showing up. Our young volunteers are the hands and hearts behind every effort — learning, growing, and building empathy through direct service.

Guided by the spirit of Aloha — love, peace, and compassion — we carry forward a mission that is both simple and profound: to leave every corner of our world a little cleaner, a little kinder, and a little more alive.
